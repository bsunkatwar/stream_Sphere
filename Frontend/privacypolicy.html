This Privacy Policy explains how StreamSphere Ltd collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Platform. By using StreamSphere, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you register, we collect your name, email address, username, and hashed password. Profile additions (bio, avatar, social links) are optional. When contacting support, we collect your message and any attachments.

1.2 Content You Upload

Videos, thumbnails, subtitles, and associated metadata (titles, descriptions, tags, embedded GPS) are stored on our servers to provide the streaming service.

1.3 Usage Data

We automatically collect IP address, browser type, OS, device identifiers, referral URLs, pages visited, watch time, clicks, and search queries to maintain reliability and improve UX.

1.4 Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, pixel tags, and local storage to recognize you, maintain sessions, measure ads, and personalize content. See our Cookies Policy for full details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to: create and manage your account; provide, operate, and improve the Platform; personalize your content feed; process transactions; send transactional and security emails; respond to support requests; monitor usage trends; detect fraud and policy violations; and comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties for their own marketing purposes.

3. Legal Bases for Processing

For users in the EEA or UK, we process personal data under: contract performance (to provide the service you requested); legitimate interests (to prevent fraud and improve the Platform); legal obligation (to comply with applicable law); and consent (for optional marketing communications, withdrawable at any time).

4. Sharing of Information

We share data with service providers (cloud hosts, payment processors, analytics vendors, CDNs) under strict data processing agreements. We may disclose information if required by law, to protect safety, or in connection with a business transaction.

Your public profile and Public videos are visible to all users and may be indexed by search engines. Adjust visibility in Studio → Settings → Privacy.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as your account is active. If you close your account, personal data is deleted or anonymized within 90 days. Uploaded video content is permanently removed within 30 days of account deletion, unless subject to a legal hold.

6. Data Security

We employ TLS encryption in transit, AES-256 encryption at rest, role-based access controls, and regular security audits. In the event of a breach likely to risk your rights, we will notify you and the relevant supervisory authority as required by law.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to: access your data; rectify inaccuracies; request erasure; restrict or object to processing; data portability; and withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, email privacy@streamsphere.io. We respond within 30 days. You may also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

8. Children's Privacy

The Platform is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover such data has been collected without parental consent, we will delete it immediately. Contact us at privacy@streamsphere.io if you have concerns.

9. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence. Where required, we implement Standard Contractual Clauses or other approved safeguards to ensure your data remains protected.

10. Policy Changes

We may update this Policy periodically. Material changes will be announced via a prominent Platform notice and, where appropriate, by email. Your continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance.
