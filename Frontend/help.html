Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

StreamSphere supports MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, and WebM. For best results we recommend MP4 with H.264 encoding. The maximum file size is 10 GB per upload. Videos are automatically transcoded to 4K, 1080p, 720p, 480p, and 360p within minutes.

You need at least 1,000 subscribers and 4,000 watch-hours in the past 12 months. Once eligible, go to Dashboard → Monetisation → Apply. Approval typically takes 3–5 business days.

Go to Studio → Videos, click the pencil icon on any video, and choose Public, Unlisted, or Private under "Visibility". You can also schedule a video to go public at a specific time.

Click Forgot Password? on the login screen, enter your email, and a reset link will arrive within 5 minutes. Check your spam folder if needed. Reset links expire after 30 minutes.

Click the ⋮ menu on any video, comment, or profile and select "Report". Choose a category and add context. Our Trust & Safety team reviews all reports within 24 hours.

Payments are processed on the 15th of each month for the previous calendar month's earnings, provided your balance exceeds £50. Payouts are made via bank transfer or PayPal based on your preference in Settings.
